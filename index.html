Spot Commerce

Tai Cheong Egg Tart @ Takashimaya

Jin Xiang Tan / YouTube
Located at the food street of Takashimaya at B1, we were lucky to avoid the massive queue as we went there around 5-6pm. While cheese tarts are the 'in' thing now, don't forget the traditional egg tarts. Each egg tart is freshly baked and best to enjoy immediately after getting your hands on them.

Nasi Lemak King @ Route 12

Jin Xiang Tan / YouTube
Located at Sam Leong Road beside Mustafa Centre, Route 12 serves your not so ordinary Nasi Lemak. You may want to bring someone along to share the platter as they come in 2 pax or 3-4 pax portion. Inspired by his road trip to Thailand, the owner decided to upsize our local delicacy to another level.
